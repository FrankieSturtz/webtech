Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive enhancement is a paradigm that all focuses on the content of a webpage. The reason why we as creators make webpages is for the users to see the content we make. With progressive enhancement, it makes the process of web development easier to digest and allows us to make a good webpage for the user's experience. Progressive enhancement is important in this day and age because it considers the user's needs. Without it, websites may not properly function due to code not properly being validated or the cause of glitches.
